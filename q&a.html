What is the impact of renewable energy on the environment?

Renewable energy significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions, mitigating climate change and air pollution. It also decreases reliance on non-renewable resources, preserving ecosystems. While manufacturing and disposal have some environmental impact, it is far lower than that of fossil fuels.

How does solar energy work?

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells. These cells generate a flow of electric current when exposed to sunlight.

What are the advantages of wind energy?

Wind energy is clean, renewable, and abundant. It doesn't produce harmful emissions, and it can be generated in areas with high wind speeds.

What are the challenges with using renewable energy?

Some challenges include the intermittency of sources like solar and wind, initial infrastructure costs, and the need for energy storage solutions to provide a continuous energy supply.

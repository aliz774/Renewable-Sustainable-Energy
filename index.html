Common Sources of Renewable Energy

Solar Energy

Solar panels use photovoltaic cells to convert sunlight into electricity. They are installed on rooftops or solar farms, ideal in regions with high sun exposure.

Wind Energy

Wind turbines capture kinetic energy from the wind. Onshore and offshore turbines have evolved with taller designs and wider blades to produce more electricity.
